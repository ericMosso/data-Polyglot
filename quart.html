Transforming the data from the database and outputting to a table.

?@tbl-bpQuery

  Heart.Disease BP_AVERAGE
1       Absence   128.8667
2      Presence   134.4417

What does that translate to in SQL syntax?

<SQL>
SELECT "Heart.Disease", AVG("BP") AS "BP_AVERAGE"
FROM "heartDF"
GROUP BY "Heart.Disease"

Formatted output table with kable() looks cleaner than a tibble.

Heart.Disease BP_AVERAGE
Absence 128.8667
Presence 134.4417

We collect() the results by executing the queries above. Queries are not actually executed on the database until now.

(dtplyr authors 2023)“A lazy data.table lazy captures the intent of dplyr verbs, only actually performing computation when requested (with collect(), pull(), as.data.frame(), data.table::as.data.table(), or tibble::as_tibble().”
